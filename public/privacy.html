Privacy & Security Policy

Last Updated: Neural Cycle V4

■ 1. Minimal Telemetry

Our SaaS does not track your PII (Personally Identifiable Information). We only collect anonymized device hardware signatures (Brand, Model, RAM) explicitly provided by you to calculate the optimal neural matrix.

■ 2. Vendor Encryption

Vendors and organizations are protected securely via encrypted tokens. Usage bounds and access controls are isolated per tenant to prevent unauthorized scraping or access code modification.

■ 3. Local Device Usage

We leverage local storage to save your visual preferences and UI states strictly to prevent repetitive server syncing, keeping the engine at extreme performance.
